About Us

We are a provider of fitness centers for beginners and advanced. With a wide variety of international facilities we can compete with fitness centers in Jakarta. Unravel had the thought of creating a fitness center that could be accessed by the general public, and finally Unravel Gym was founded in 2019 in Jakarta.

Comfort for people is Unravel's vision in developing a fitness center. Our mission this year is to become a gym that provides the best service from previous years. With the best service, people will be more interested in exercising in our gym.

Class

We offer two classes at this gym. Classes that can be taken are beginner and advanced classes to practice.

We guarantee proper training for both classes. And we also offer a free consultation for every class.

Beginner Class

Beginner Class, a class that offers exercises for beginners who want to train in our gym. In this class there will be light exercises first and then over time the exercises will be added to the difficulty.

This class focuses on making beginners advanced in fitness training. With regular training and being guided by a trainer, it is hoped that this beginner class will be a solution for those who want to start fitness training.

Advanced Class

Advanced class, In this class everyone has built their basic muscles and wants to continue to increase muscle mass and fitness.
